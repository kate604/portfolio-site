About

My Story

After working as a photographer and editor for many years, I started looking for a fulfilling new challenge and came to UX design. I was drawn to the mixture of creativity and practicality, as well as the potential to make really useful products for people. While I’m still early in my journey, I look forward to continuing to learn and grow in this field.

I love to travel and have lived abroad in the past - South America is high up on my “to see” list. Another hobby is watching films as I find a lot of visual inspiration through them.
